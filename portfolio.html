    Portfolio

    Welcome to my portfolio

    UX Projects

    TicketCinX

    TicketCinX is a user-friendly mobile ticketing app designed to simplify the movie-going experience. It offers a sleek interface for effortless browsing of the latest movie releases, showtimes, and nearby theaters. What sets TicketCinX apart is its advanced recommendation engine, which analyzes your viewing history and genre preferences to provide personalized movie suggestions that align perfectly with your taste.

    VetShed

    VetShed is a specialized platform dedicated to optimizing employee management in the veterinary field. It is meticulously crafted to enhance the efficiency of veterinary clinics and hospitals by simplifying workforce management tasks, particularly in the area of staff scheduling. Our user-friendly platform is designed to make the complex task of managing employees in the veterinary industry a seamless and effortless process, ultimately improving overall clinic operations.

    MedLink

    MedLink is a groundbreaking mobile app designed to transform healthcare accessibility. Our platform caters to individuals of all ages, offering convenient and streamlined access to vital medications and healthcare services. Whether you need prescriptions, medical advice, or essential healthcare resources, MedLink is the one-stop solution for effortless and efficient healthcare support.

    Web Development Projects

    Museum Of Australian Democracy At Old Parliament House

    The Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House showcases the rich history and democratic heritage of Australia. Powered by Drupal, their website serves as an interactive platform connecting visitors to a wealth of information, engaging exhibits, and educational resources.

    IDEAL Plan Management

    IDEAL Plan Management comprises a team of seasoned NDIS plan managers dedicated to serving clients across Australia. The comprehensive services are seamlessly integrated into a user-friendly Drupal-based website, ensuring accessibility and efficiency throughout the NDIS journey.

    Alcohol Tobacco & Other Drug Association ACT

    The ACT Alcohol, Tobacco, and Other Drug (ATOD) Program Directory serves as a comprehensive resource showcasing the wide array of programs offered within the ACT ATOD sector. This directory is meticulously curated and presented through a custom WordPress theme, ensuring an organized and accessible platform for individuals seeking information about these vital services.

    International Needs Australia

    International Needs Australia is dedicated to addressing the pressing needs of the world's most vulnerable populations. The mission takes to some of the most underserved and marginalized communities in Eastern Africa, South Asia, Southeast Asia, and the Pacific region. As part of the commitment to transparency and accessibility, the website is developed using a customized WordPress theme, ensuring that our message and impact reach a global audience.

    Oxfam Aotearoa

    Oxfam Aotearoa is a prominent charitable organization based in New Zealand that operates as part of the international Oxfam confederation. The website is skillfully crafted using WordPress and Elementor, a powerful website builder plugin for WordPress. This choice of technology ensures a user-friendly and visually appealing online platform to engage their audience and advance their mission of creating positive change in the world.

    Canberra Property Partners

    Canberra Property Partner is a real estate and property management company based in Canberra, Australia. The website is designed and managed using HubSpot, a versatile marketing and customer relationship management (CRM) platform. This choice of technology allows Canberra Property Partner to efficiently manage their digital presence, engage with clients, and provide valuable information to individuals seeking property-related services in the Canberra area.

    Police Week

    Police Week is an online platform dedicated to commemorating and celebrating the contributions and sacrifices of law enforcement officers. This website has been thoughtfully developed using WordPress and Elementor, a versatile WordPress plugin for building and customizing websites. The use of Elementor ensures an interactive and engaging online experience for visitors, allowing them to explore and participate in Police Week activities while paying tribute to the dedication of police officers.

    National Rock Garden

    National Rock Garden is a unique and captivating institution dedicated to celebrating the geological heritage of a country. It typically showcases a diverse collection of rocks, minerals, and geological formations native to the region it represents. The website is thoughtfully designed and developed using a customized WordPress theme. This approach ensures that the digital platform effectively communicates the fascinating world of geology to a broad audience, making information about the geological wonders of the nation easily accessible and engaging.

    Aircraft Noise Ombudsman

    The Aircraft Noise Ombudsman is an independent office or authority established in Australia to address and manage concerns related to aircraft noise pollution. The website is thoughtfully designed and developed using a customized WordPress theme. This choice of technology allows for an efficient and accessible online platform where affected individuals can find information, report concerns, and access resources to better understand and mitigate the impact of aircraft noise in their communities.

    iMeet!

    iMeet! is a go-to online meeting platform designed to streamline the meeting organization process. With a user-friendly interface developed using ReactJS and Spring Boot, iMeet! empowers users to effortlessly schedule meetings, collaborate on agendas in advance, and access a library of meeting templates for efficient planning. Its intuitive drag-and-drop functionality and seamless online cooperation features make it the ideal tool for productive and engaging meetings, ensuring that every gathering is a success.
